# **App Name**: Aluplan Digital

## Core Features:

- Mega Menu Navigation: Full-width mega menu dynamically populated from Payload CMS, displayed below the header, showing solutions and products in a 4-column responsive card grid. Each card includes an icon, title, and short description. Smooth fade + slide-down animation with subtle hover lift effect and shadow.
- Solutions Catalog: Browse and filter solutions based on categories such as Bina Tasarımı, Altyapı Tasarımı, İş Birliği, İnşaat Planlaması, Eklentiler, Diğer Çözümler, dynamically pulled from Payload CMS.
- Product Catalog: Explore product offerings with details from Payload CMS, including versions (Basic, Concept, Professional, Ultimate, Civil, Precast), related solutions, and descriptions.
- Education & Support Resources: Access webinars, certifications, and events, dynamically listed from Payload CMS. Includes links to recorded webinars, training videos, and certification information.
- Customer Success Stories: Read case studies showcasing successful implementations, filtered by region (Global / Türkiye) and discipline (Structural, Civil, Bridge, Precast), with rich content, images, and videos sourced from Payload CMS.
- Resource Download Center: Download resources such as FAQs, tutorials, licenses, and Allplan Connect links. Managed via Payload CMS for dynamic updates.
- Dynamic Content Tool: Employ an AI tool to evaluate new case studies against a corpus of successful examples and advise on optimal keywords and metadata for Payload CMS entries.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Blue (#3F78A5) for headers, main buttons, and links.
- Background color: White (#FFFFFF) for overall page background to maintain a clean and modern look.
- Accent color: Orange (#ED7D31) for call-to-actions, hover highlights, and interactive elements.
- Success / Positive indicators: Green (#70AD47) for KPI cards, success messages, and positive notifications.
- Neutral / Text color: Dark gray (#445354) for body text, subtitles, and secondary information.
- Font: 'Inter', modern sans-serif for headlines and body text.
- Maintain clear hierarchy between headings, subheadings, and body content.
- Fully responsive, grid-based layouts optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- Maintain consistent spacing, padding, and container widths.
- Cards, lists, and tables adapt to screen size with proper stacking on mobile.
- Subtle fade and slide transitions for mega menus, modals, and cards.
- Hover lift, shadow, and color change effects for interactive items.
- Parallax effect in hero sections or featured banners.
- Use a consistent set of recognizable icons aligned with brand identity.
- Icons must enhance comprehension and guide users through content hierarchy.
